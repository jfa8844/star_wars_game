# Star Wars: Hyperspace Survivor

A thrilling HTML5 Canvas 2D scrolling shooter set in the Star Wars universe!

## Features
- **Dynamic Graphics**: Enjoy pseudo-3D graphics with gradients, particle effects, and blooming lasers.
- **Three Levels of Intensity**:
    1. **Asteroid Field**: Survive waves of TIE fighters.
    2. **Fleet Battle**: Engage massive Star Destroyers alongside allied X-Wings.
    3. **Trench Run**: Navigate the Death Star trench, avoid wall turrets, and fire proton torpedoes into the exhaust port!
- **Unlockable Skins (The Hangar)**:
    - **X-Wing** (Default)
    - **TIE Fighter** (Defeat 25 TIEs)
    - **B-Wing** (Defeat 50 TIEs)
    - **Millennium Falcon** (Clear the Death Star 2 times)
    - **Star Destroyer** (Clear the Death Star 5 times)
    - **Death Star** (Defeat 150 TIEs and clear the Death Star 10 times)

## Controls
- **Left Arrow (`←`)**: Move Left
- **Right Arrow (`→`)**: Move Right
- **Spacebar (`SPACE`)**: Fire blasters (uses ammo)

## Power-ups
- **Ammo Drops**: Collect glowing green battery cells to reload your blasters!

## How to Play
Just double-click the `index.html` file to open it in any modern web browser! No installation or servers required.

## Progression Tracking
Your progress (total TIEs destroyed and total Death Stars cleared) is saved directly to your browser's local storage. You can safely close the game and pick up where you left off later! To reset your progress, click the "RESET PROGRESS" button in the Hangar menu.
